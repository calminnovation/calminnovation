Privacy Policy for Upkeep

Effective Date: December 25, 2024

Introduction

At Calm Innovation, we believe your habits and personal growth data are private. This Privacy Policy applies to the Upkeep mobile application (the "App"). We built Upkeep to be offline-first and privacy-by-design.

Data Collection and Usage

We do not collect, store, or share your personal data.

  • Offline Storage: All data you enter into Upkeep (habits, logs, notes) is stored locally on your device using an encrypted local database.
  • No Servers: We do not have servers that receive or process your habit data.
  • No Analytics: We do not use third-party analytics services to track your usage behavior.
  • No Accounts: You do not need to create an account to use Upkeep.

Data Sovereignty & Backup

Since your data is stored on your device, you are responsible for it. Upkeep provides functionality to Export your data to a JSON file. You can use this file to back up your data or transfer it to another device. We cannot access or recover your data if you lose your device or uninstall the app without a backup.

Permissions

Notifications: To send you reminders for your habits. These are scheduled locally on your device.

Children's Privacy

Our Application does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from anyone.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
